Grants, loans & programs for US farmers

Emergency Relief Program (ERP) 2025
USDA — Farm Service Agency
Up to $125,000
Financial assistance to eligible farmers and ranchers who suffered losses due to qualifying emergency events including wildfires, drought, flooding, and other natural disasters.
🌾 Grant ⚡ Urgent 🇺🇸 Federal
📅 Deadline: April 30, 2026
🏦
FSA Farm Operating Loan
USDA — Farm Service Agency
Up to $400,000
Low-interest loans for family farmers to cover operating expenses, livestock, farm equipment, seed, feed, and other short-term needs. Priority given to beginning farmers.
🏦 Loan Low Interest
📅 Rolling applications
🌱
Beginning Farmer & Rancher Development
USDA — National Institute of Food & Agriculture
Up to $250,000
Grants to support training, education, outreach and technical assistance for beginning farmers and ranchers. Includes mentorship programs and land access support.
🌾 Grant 🎓 Education
📅 Deadline: May 15, 2026
☔
Crop Insurance — MPCI Program
USDA — Risk Management Agency
Protects up to 85% of crop value
Multi-Peril Crop Insurance protects against natural disasters including drought, excessive moisture, hail, wind, frost, and plant disease. Subsidized premiums available.
📋 Insurance Subsidized
📅 Sign-up: March 15 cutoff for most crops
♻️
EQIP — Environmental Quality Incentives
USDA — Natural Resources Conservation Service
Up to $450,000 over 10 years
Financial and technical assistance for conservation practices including irrigation efficiency, nutrient management, and conservation cover. One of the most funded USDA programs.
🌾 Grant 🌿 Conservation
📅 Continuous sign-up
